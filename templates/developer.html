http://localhost:5000/restaurants/JSON:
Lists all the restaurants

http://localhost:5000/restaurants/restaurant_id/menu/JSON:
Lists all the menu's of the respective restaurant (restaurant_id is an integer)

http://localhost:5000/restaurants/restaurant_id/menu/menu_id/JSON:
Lists an individual menu pertaining to a certain restaurant (restaurant_id is an integer , menu_id is an integer)
